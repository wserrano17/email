SOCKS

I went to this sock store and got socks for running because I keep getting blisters. But the socks are special because they have a lifetime warranty. With the socks, I was able to run all the way up to Greenlake and ran for almost an hour! I only stopped because my blisters were popping off. :curses: The socks still helped though. I think my kirkland signature socks would have shredded my soles. Running is so fun when ur not on the mt lebanon high school cross country team. Also sidenote: why do I look snatched in this photo?? post run buccal fat removal i guess!
